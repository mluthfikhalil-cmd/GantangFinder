## 🚨 IMPORTANT REMINDERS

1. **Consistency is key** - Better 1 video/day for 30 days than 30 videos in 1 day

2. **Engagement > Numbers** - 100 engaged followers better than 1000 ghost followers

3. **Community first** - Help the community, don't just promote

4. **Track everything** - If you don't measure, you can't improve

5. **Learn from data** - Analytics show what's working, what's not

6. **Be patient** - Marketing takes time. First viral video usually takes weeks

7. **Quality > Quantity** - 1 great video > 10 mediocre videos
